Brad & Lisa's Household Portfolio
Investment Strategy

A self-directed three-pillar retirement strategy spanning 7 accounts and over 60 holdings. The three pillars are not separate accounts — they are virtual designations applied across our existing Schwab and Fidelity accounts, with each position permanently assigned to a pillar and managed accordingly.

Pillar 1 — Income generates passive dividend income across 6 accounts — no principal sold, dividends redeployed until retirement.
Pillar 2 — Growth is Brad's and Lisa's 401k accounts compounding untouched until RMDs begin in 2035.
Pillar 3 — Health/LTC is a self-funded reserve held virtually inside the IRAs, fully replacing traditional LTC insurance and providing a dedicated pool for future healthcare and miscellaneous medical expenses.
